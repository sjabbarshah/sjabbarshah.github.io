Offshore wind turbines operate in extremely harsh environments, making regular maintenance highly complex. In collaboration with Arrow SEA, we conducted a three-day field visit to offshore wind turbines deep in the Atlantic Ocean. The trip combined drone-based visual inspections of turbine blades and real-time telemetry sensor setups.

During this campaign, I was awarded the **Leadership Award** for coordinating the crack detection project, successfully delivering a pipeline that improves defect identification speed and aids technicians in structural health decision-making.

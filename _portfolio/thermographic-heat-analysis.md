Heating and cooling account for a massive portion of structural energy consumption. This project focuses on utilizing advanced infrared thermography to map building heat loss. By capturing spatial-thermal variations across building envelopes under controlled environmental gradients, our lab mapped insulation deficits and draft zones. 

The developed workflows help civil inspectors quickly assess building energy efficiency in a non-destructive manner. The work was highlighted and presented at the ClimaTech conference in Boston, MA.

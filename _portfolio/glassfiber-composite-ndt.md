Glassfiber-reinforced polymer composites are extensively used in structural industries, including wind turbine blades. However, internal manufacturing delaminations and impacts are difficult to detect visually. This project introduces a Principal Component Thermography Analysis (PCTA) workflow.

By applying pulsed thermal excitation and capturing transient thermal decay, we extract principal component features that isolate deep subsurface anomalies from surface noise. We presented this advanced NDT workflow to industry leaders and board members at the Windstar IAB Meeting in June 2026.

I attended the **ClimaTech** conference in Boston, MA, on May 4, 2026. The event featured cutting-edge presentations on climate-focused technologies, networking with industry leaders, and discussions on renewable energy policy. Highlights included a keynote on advanced wind-farm monitoring and a workshop on deploying low-cost solar micro-grids. Additionally, our lab introduced our current thermographic heat analysis of buildings and their energy efficiency.

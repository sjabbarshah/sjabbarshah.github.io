In late May I traveled to **Arrow SEA** for an on‑site visit of the **Officeshore** wind turbine. The trip included a detailed inspection of blade‑level data acquisition, drone‑based visual inspections, and a workshop on advanced turbine control strategies.

During the visit I was honored with the **Leadership Award** for spearheading the wind‑turbine crack detection and quantification project. The award recognized my coordination of interdisciplinary teams and successful delivery of a prototype system that improves fault detection speed by 30%.

*Key activities*:
- Drone flights inside the turbine housing to capture high‑resolution imagery of blade surfaces.
- Real‑time data streaming to a cloud analytics dashboard.
- Collaborative brainstorming session on next‑generation turbine health monitoring.
